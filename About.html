About MaeScrapBook

MaeScrapBook is a place you can explore original Short Stories, Poems, and Artworks,
Whether you're bored, interested, or just curiuos do enjoy you're time here.

So here you are, my digital scrapbook of each single moment that I consider unforgettable
and thus deserving to be saved. Originally, this project was built for myself to create a
memoir of the happy, messy, and downright silly experiences I’ve had. Every page is a
scrapbook of special days. In this digital collection, I have used pictures, videos, and
music to recreated those feelings that one gets when remembering those moments. To me it’s
not just a picture gallery, it’s a story, a poem, an artwork and every item here, is an
emotion of that particular time.
