Poems

Garden of Thoughts
In the garden of my mind,
Thoughts grow wild and intertwine,
Some bloom brightly, some are shy,
All of them are mine.
Raindrops fall and make me doubt,
Sunshine helps me figure out,
Every thought’s a tiny seed,
In this garden, they’re all I need.

Echoes of Silence
When everything goes quiet,
And the world seems to pause,
I hear the echoes of our hearts,
In the silent space we cause.
Eyes speak what words cannot say,
Love lingers in this quiet way,
In the calm, we find our own,
Where silent whispers are softly known.

Whispers
Cold steel,
whispered breathe,
A promise made,
A dance with death.

Reflections
The mirror shows more than just a face,
It tells a story, in its own space,
Of who we are and who we dream,
Of all the things that might have been.
Reflections capture truths and lies,
In the way we look, and in our eyes,
It’s not just how we appear to be,
But also what’s hidden, deep within.
